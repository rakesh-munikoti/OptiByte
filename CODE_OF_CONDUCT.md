# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

- **Be respectful.** Treat everyone with dignity and respect.
- **Be mindful of language.** Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.
- **Be collaborative.** Focus on constructive feedback and inclusive collaboration.

## Enforcement

Instances of harassment or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly. The maintainers are obligated to keep the community safe and will take appropriate actions, which may include a warning, temporary ban, or permanent removal from the project.
